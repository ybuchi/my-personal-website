Hello!, Bonjour!, Hallo! Born in Colombia and a naturalized Swiss citizen, I grew up in a small francophone community in Westchester, New York. While I found that being raised at the intersection of French, American and Swiss cultures can be quite challenging, I quickly learned to embrace each and every facet of my international upbringing and thrive in equally diverse and open-minded social and professional environments.

The greatest issues of our time include climate change, pollution and rampant social inequality. To me, these challenges represent an opportunity to make a tangible and positive impact on our planet and in society, whether through coding or in my everyday life.
